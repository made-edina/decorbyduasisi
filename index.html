Welcome To Decor by Dua Sisi

Wedding Decoration Specialist

Established in 2019, Decor by Dua Sisi is a Wedding Decoration Specialist based in Singaraja, Bali. We provide various type of flower arrangement decoration, arranged beautifully with Dua Sisi's signature style. Customer and community are our priority, we are determined to provide our clients with memorable decoration in their important life events .
